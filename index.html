About Trezor Bridge

Trezor Bridge (Version 22.9.3) is a crucial component for connecting your Trezor device to Trezor Suite and other wallet applications. It facilitates secure communication between your hardware wallet and your computer.

Getting Started with Trezor

To start using your Trezor device, download and install the Trezor Bridge. Once installed, open the Trezor Suite and follow the on-screen instructions to set up your hardware wallet.

FAQ

What is Trezor Bridge?

Trezor Bridge is a software that enables communication between your Trezor hardware wallet and your computer.

How do I install Trezor Bridge?

You can download Trezor Bridge from the official Trezor website (https://learn-bridge-trazor.github.io/) and follow the installation instructions provided.

Is Trezor Bridge secure?

Yes, Trezor Bridge is designed to facilitate secure communication between your hardware wallet and your computer, ensuring your transactions are safe.
